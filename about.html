Our Story

The Waffizz Villa was born from a simple dream: to bring authentic, mouth-watering waffles to Indore. What began as a small kiosk has grown into a beloved destination where families gather to enjoy crispy waffles with their favorite toppings. Every waffle is made fresh to order using the finest quality ingredients.
